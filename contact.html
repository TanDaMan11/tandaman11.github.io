Contact Us

We'd Love to Hear From You

Get in Touch

Have questions about our games? Found a bug? Have a game suggestion? We'd love to hear from you! Our team reads and responds to all inquiries. Whether you want to report an issue, suggest a new feature, or just say hello, feel free to reach out using the contact information below.

Contact Information

Email: contact@tandamangames.com

Please include relevant details in your email so we can better assist you. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours during business days.

How We Can Help

Below are some of the common reasons people contact us. Whatever your inquiry, we're here to help!

🐛 Report a Bug

Found a bug or technical issue with one of our games? Please let us know! Include details about what game you were playing, what happened, what you were doing when it occurred, and what device/browser you were using. Your bug reports help us improve our games for everyone.

💡 Game Suggestions

Have an idea for a new game or a feature you'd like to see? We love hearing ideas from our community! Tell us about your game concept, what makes it fun, and why you think our players would enjoy it. We carefully consider all suggestions from our community.

🤝 Partnership & Collaboration

Interested in partnering with Tandaman Games or collaborating on a project? We're always interested in discussing potential partnerships and collaborations. Let us know what you have in mind and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

📝 Content & Media Inquiries

Are you a content creator, journalist, or media outlet interested in covering Tandaman Games? We're happy to discuss press inquiries, provide information about our platform, or work with you on coverage. Please include details about your outlet and what information you need.

📧 General Questions

Have general questions about how our games work, our privacy practices, or anything else? Feel free to send us an email with your question and we'll do our best to provide a helpful response.

💬 Feedback & Comments

We value your feedback! Tell us what you love about Tandaman Games, what we could improve, or any other comments you have. Your input helps us create a better experience for all our players.

What to Include in Your Email

To help us respond to your inquiry as quickly and accurately as possible, please include:

  • A clear subject line describing your inquiry
  • Your name (or username if you prefer)
  • A detailed description of your issue, suggestion, or question
  • If reporting a bug: the game name, what you were doing, what happened, and your device/browser info
  • Any relevant screenshots or additional information that might help
  • The best way to contact you if we need more information

Response Time

We work hard to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible. While we aim to respond within 48 hours, during peak times responses may take a bit longer. We appreciate your patience and thank you for your interest in Tandaman Games!

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is important to us. Any personal information you provide when contacting us will be handled according to our Privacy Policy. We will never share your information with third parties without your consent, and we use appropriate security measures to protect your data.

Thank You!

Thank you for being part of the Tandaman Games community! We look forward to hearing from you and working together to make Tandaman Games the best gaming platform it can be. Whether you have feedback, a question, or just want to say hi, we'd love to hear from you at contact@tandamangames.com.
